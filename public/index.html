Humans and Livestock

This data visualisation gives insights about the extents of global meat production. It compares human population with the amount of animal meat that gets produced on each continent for agriculture in the year 2016. You can hover over each bar to get more info displayed.

The animal meat data relate to animals slaughtered within national boundaries, irrespective of their origin. All data shown relate to total meat production, that is, from both commercial and farm slaughter. Data are given in terms of dressed carcass weight, excluding offal and slaughter fats.

Visualisation Data gathered from FAOSTAT.
